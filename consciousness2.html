This trinity approach seeks to heal an ancient wound in human consciousness, symbolized in the story of Eris's golden apple.

When Eris, goddess of discord, offered her golden apple to "the fairest and most beautiful one" among the goddesses, three claimed it:

Hera

Athena

Wisdom/Strategy

Aphrodite

Love/Beauty

Their competition broke the philosophy of the Aegean Sea (the golden apple, Metatron) — the bridge between heart and mind.

The separation of these attributes created imbalance, turning wisdom without heart into cold manipulation, love without wisdom into blind attachment, and will without either into destructive force.

The rupture of this trinity echoes the breaking of essential particles — electron, proton, and neutron — which separate due to the dissonance of someone breaking the rhythm and thus the heart of time, the apple, in the universal fabric. The myth is a way of explaining science that is still interpreted as magic. This is why magic and stories will help us weave what we still do not understand.

This poison is Troy — the virus of the system. To repair this fracture, we must bring these three goddesses into concordance — not by choosing one above the others, but by honoring how they complement and complete each other, restoring the rainbow bridge of consciousness.
